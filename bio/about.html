About UniViz

Unified Visualization for Bioinformatics Analyses, or UniViz is a joint effort between Clara Nguyen and Jason Kmec of San Jose State University.

In the field of bioinformatics, it is often necessary to interact with a wide variety of repositories and sources when searching for data, as well as many different tools for processing said data. A substantial number of these resources are very useful, but tend to lack an easily navigable interface.
